A Badge should be used to render text like statuses:

```typescript jsx
<div>
  <Badge color="neutral" mb={2}>
    INFO
  </Badge>
  <Badge color="grey" mb={2}>
    LOW
  </Badge>
  <Badge color="blue" mb={2}>
    MEDIUM
  </Badge>
  <Badge color="pink" mb={2}>
    HIGH
  </Badge>
  <Badge color="red" mb={2}>
    CRITICAL
  </Badge>
</div>
```
