An Iconbutton can be solid and have different sizes:

```typescript jsx
<Box>
  <SimpleGrid columns={3} gap={4} mb={2}>
    <Box>
      <IconButton size="small" icon="user" aria-label="User" />
    </Box>
    <Box>
      <IconButton size="small" icon="user" variantColor="teal-500" aria-label="User" />
    </Box>
    <Box>
      <IconButton
        size="small"
        icon="user"
        variantColor="violet-400"
        variantBorderStyle="circle"
        aria-label="User"
      />
    </Box>
  </SimpleGrid>
  <SimpleGrid columns={3} gap={4} mb={2}>
    <Box>
      <IconButton size="medium" icon="user" aria-label="User" />
    </Box>
    <Box>
      <IconButton size="medium" icon="user" variantColor="teal-500" aria-label="User" />
    </Box>
    <Box>
      <IconButton
        size="medium"
        icon="user"
        variantColor="violet-400"
        variantBorderStyle="circle"
        aria-label="User"
      />
    </Box>
  </SimpleGrid>
  <SimpleGrid columns={3} gap={4}>
    <Box>
      <IconButton icon="user" aria-label="User" />
    </Box>
    <Box>
      <IconButton icon="user" variantColor="teal-500" aria-label="User" />
    </Box>
    <Box>
      <IconButton
        icon="user"
        variantColor="violet-400"
        variantBorderStyle="circle"
        aria-label="User"
      />
    </Box>
  </SimpleGrid>
</Box>
```

An IconButton can be `ghost`:

```typescript jsx
<SimpleGrid columns={4} gap={4}>
  <IconButton variant="ghost" icon="user" aria-label="User" variantColor="navyblue-300" />
  <IconButton
    variant="ghost"
    icon="user"
    aria-label="User"
    variantColor="teal-500"
    variantBorderStyle="circle"
  />
  <IconButton variant="ghost" icon="user" aria-label="User" variantColor="navyblue-300" active />
  <IconButton
    variant="ghost"
    icon="user"
    aria-label="User"
    variantColor="teal-500"
    variantBorderStyle="circle"
    active
  />
</SimpleGrid>
```

An IconButton can be `outline`:

```typescript jsx
<SimpleGrid columns={4} gap={4}>
  <IconButton variant="outline" icon="user" aria-label="User" variantColor="navyblue-300" />
  <IconButton
    variant="outline"
    icon="user"
    aria-label="User"
    variantColor="teal-500"
    variantBorderStyle="circle"
  />
  <IconButton variant="outline" icon="user" aria-label="User" variantColor="navyblue-300" active />
  <IconButton
    variant="outline"
    icon="user"
    aria-label="User"
    variantColor="teal-500"
    variantBorderStyle="circle"
    active
  />
</SimpleGrid>
```

An IconButton can have no styling:

```typescript jsx
<SimpleGrid columns={4} gap={4}>
  <IconButton variant="unstyled" icon="user" aria-label="User" />
  <IconButton variant="unstyled" icon="user" aria-label="User" variantBorderStyle="circle" />
</SimpleGrid>
```

An IconButton can have a colored icon:

```typescript jsx
<SimpleGrid columns={4} gap={4}>
  <IconButton icon="user" variantColor="navyblue-300" iconColor="teal-500" aria-label="User" />
  <IconButton
    variant="ghost"
    icon="user"
    variantColor="navyblue-300"
    iconColor="teal-500"
    aria-label="User"
  />
  <IconButton
    variant="outline"
    icon="user"
    variantColor="navyblue-300"
    iconColor="teal-500"
    aria-label="User"
  />
  <IconButton variant="unstyled" icon="user" aria-label="User" iconColor="teal-500" />
</SimpleGrid>
```
